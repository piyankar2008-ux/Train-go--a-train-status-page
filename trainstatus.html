    How Live Train Info Works

    How We Get Live Train Info

    Our train status system pulls real-time data from NTES (National Train Enquiry System), which is the official source for live train movement across Indian Railways.

    When you search for a train, our backend requests this data through a structured method, processes the raw information, and shows you clean details like current location, expected arrival/departure, delays, and platform number.

    This allows users to get instant updates — similar to the apps like “Where is My Train” or “Trainman” — but in a simple format, made with purpose.

    Accuracy: Since this info comes directly from railway systems, it's mostly accurate — around 85% to 95% on average. Occasionally, small delays can happen due to poor station network or lag in updates.
